Old CCFinder

CCFinder is a code-clone detector. The first version was implemented in 1999 and published in 2001. The final version has been released in March 2005.
→ The CCFinder Homepage

CloneTracker

CloneTracker (developed by By A. Lozano) measures the amount (i.e. number) and density (i.e. amount per time unit) of change in methods while they have a cloned code snippet versus while they do not. It also measures the amount and density of co-change in pairs of methods while they share a cloned code snippet versus while they do not.
